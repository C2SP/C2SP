The test vectors in rsa.py were generated by a community effort, where
volunteers contributed an
[estimated](https://claude.ai/share/323de949-9849-4846-b406-10d0ba0f4508)
65 core-years (95% CI: 38.0–91.4 core-years) of CPU time, peaking at more than
5,300 cores (95% CI: 2,900–7,700 cores).

Volunteers were recruited privately, and on
[Bluesky](https://bsky.app/profile/filippo.abyssdomain.expert/post/3mbmbk3siep2c)
and [Mastodon](https://abyssdomain.expert/@filippo/115837613871465307), and
included, among many others:
